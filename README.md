# ⚖️ Frame-Gym: AI Dating Simulator & Sandbox

An interactive web application built with Python, Streamlit, and the Google Gemini API. **Frame-Gym** is a conversational simulator designed to help users practice maintaining their "frame", handling shit-tests, and mastering the "Gentle Dom" dynamic in dating app text conversations.

> ⚠️ **LANGUAGE NOTICE:** The UI, system prompts, and AI outputs are currently natively in **Italian**. English and full multi-language support are planned for future releases!

## 🚀 Features

* 🤖 **Dynamic AI Persona:** At the start of each session, the AI generates a randomized dating profile (Name, Age, Tinder-style passions, Bio, and Photo descriptions).
* ⚖️ **The Umpire System:** The AI doesn't just chat; it evaluates you. If you act needy, lose your temper, over-apologize, or break frame, the system triggers a **`[GAME OVER]`**, providing a clinical analysis of your mistake and a "Master Chat" example of how you should have handled it.
* 🎲 **Stochastic Mood Engine:** Real-time sliders allow you to adjust the probability of the AI's mood. Force the AI to be *Weird*, *Cold/Boring*, or *Enthusiastic* on the fly to test your adaptability under different conversational pressures.
* 🦇 **The Gothifier (Beta):** A custom toggle that overrides standard generation, forcing the AI to create profiles belonging to the Alternative/Goth/Metal subcultures.
* 🕵️ **Split Output Vision:** The UI separates the AI's internal thoughts (`[MOOD]`) from the actual text message sent (`[MESSAGE]`), giving you a behind-the-scenes look at how your words affect her psychology.
